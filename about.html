Journey

The adventure begins in the classroom

My journey began in the classrooms of Kerala, where I discovered a deep passion for teaching, English literature and knowledge sharing. What started as a role as a college educator soon grew into something bigger ‚Äî connecting with students, shaping minds and turning learning into a lived experience.

Over time, this love for education merged with my curiosity for the world outside the classroom. I started documenting travel stories, quiz experiences and cultural journeys, which later evolved into Malayalam travel vlogs and a published travel book that bring my journeys closer to readers and viewers.
