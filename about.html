About Rijo John Sankarathil

Educator, travel vlogger and quiz master from Kerala — a journey from classroom teaching to global adventures and Guinness World Record recognition.

The Adventure Begins

My journey began in the classrooms of Kerala, where I discovered a deep passion for teaching, English literature and knowledge sharing. What started as a role as a college educator soon grew into something bigger — connecting with students, shaping minds and turning learning into a lived experience.

Over time, this love for education merged with my curiosity for the world outside the classroom. I started documenting travel stories, quiz experiences and cultural journeys, which later evolved into Malayalam travel vlogs and a published travel book that bring my journeys closer to readers and viewers.

Guinness World Record

In 2018, I achieved what many thought was impossible - setting a Guinness World Record medalist for Six hour continuous participation in Sreekandan Nair talk show and debate – held at MGM School, Kottarakkara. This achievement was not just about records or numbers — it was about bringing together thousands of young minds, celebrating curiosity and proving that learning can be exciting, inclusive and unforgettable.

"Education is not just about textbooks; it's about experiencing the world, asking questions and sharing those experiences to inspire the next generation."
